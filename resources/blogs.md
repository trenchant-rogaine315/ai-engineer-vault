# Trusted AI Engineering Blogs & Sources

Follow these 25+ trusted sources to stay technical and ignore the AI hype cycle.

---

## Essential Newsletters
- **[TLDR AI](https://tldr.tech/ai)** | Daily | Concise, technical summaries of the latest research and engineering releases.
- **[AlphaSignal](https://alphasignal.ai/)** | Weekly | Data-driven ranking of the top papers and repos that are actually gaining traction in the dev community.
- **[Latent Space](https://www.latent.space/)** | Weekly | Deep-dive podcast and newsletter focusing on the "AI Engineer" persona and tech stack.
- **[Interconnects](https://www.interconnects.ai/)** | Weekly | Nathan Lambert's technical analysis of model training, alignment, and RLHF.

- **[Anthropic Research](https://www.anthropic.com/research)** | irregular | Excellent deep dives into interpretability and Constitutional AI.
- **[Meta AI (FAIR)](https://ai.meta.com/blog/)** | regular | The primary driver of the open-weights ecosystem (Llama series).
- **[Anyscale Blog](https://www.anyscale.com/blog)** | regular | Expert-level content on scaling Ray, vLLM, and distributed inference.
- **[Vercel AI Blog](https://vercel.com/blog/tags/ai)** | regular | Focused on the intersection of front-end engineering and AI orchestration.
- **[Hugging Face Blog](https://huggingface.co/blog)** | regular | The central hub for open-source model releases, fine-tuning guides, and multimodal research.
- **[LlamaIndex Blog](https://www.llamaindex.ai/blog)** | regular | The definitive source for production RAG strategies and agentic workflow patterns.
